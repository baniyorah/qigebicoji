Jeanie Buss Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Jeanie Buss is best known as a businesswoman, being the president and part-owner of the Los Angeles Lakers professional basketball team in the National Basketball Association (NBA). Her net worth is estimated to be $20 million, which she has gained through her successful business ventures and as a model.

Born on the 26th of September, 1961, in Santa Monica, California, she comes from a wealthy family, with her father, Jerry Buss, being the majority owner of the Los Angeles Lakers and the tennis team the Los Angeles Strings. Following her parents’ divorce, she lived with her father and became deeply involved in the business, eventually taking on roles within the family’s sports ventures.

Her journey in the business world began when she was made the general manager of the Los Angeles Strings by her father at the age of 19. She continued to take on more responsibilities within the sports industry, eventually becoming the president of the Los Angeles Lakers after the passing of her father in 2013.

Apart from her successful business career, Jeanie has also dabbled in modeling, posing for Playboy in May 1995, which further contributed to her net worth.

In her personal life, she was previously married to Olympic volleyball player Steve Timmons and is currently engaged to coach Phil Jackson. She is quite active on social media, using platforms like Twitter and Facebook to connect with fans and share updates about the Lakers and her personal life.
