Rita Peet Obituary, Death – It is with profound sorrow that we announce the passing of Rita Peet, a cherished member of the Bismarck community. Rita, aged 63, departed from our midst on December 20, 2023, leaving behind a legacy of love and treasured memories that will forever be etched in the hearts of those who had the privilege of knowing her. To honor and celebrate Rita’s remarkable life, a Mass of Christian burial has been scheduled for 10 a.m. on Friday, December 29, at Spirit of Life in Mandan.

This solemn occasion serves as an opportunity for family, friends, and the community to join together, paying tribute to Rita’s life, sharing condolences, and finding solace in the collective embrace of shared memories. A visitation has been arranged on Thursday from 5 to 7 p.m. at Parkway Funeral Service, located at 2330 Tyler Parkway in Bismarck. During this time, friends and well-wishers can offer their support to the grieving family. A rosary and vigil service will follow at 7 p.m., providing a moment for reflection and prayer in remembrance of Rita’s life.

Rita Peet’s presence brought warmth, kindness, and a gentle spirit to the lives of many. Her departure creates a void that will be deeply felt by those who had the privilege of sharing in her grace. As we gather to bid our final farewells, let us remember and celebrate the beautiful soul that was Rita, finding solace in the enduring love and memories she leaves behind.
