Eminems Rap God music video reaches 1 billion views on Youtube, 6 years after its release

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Rapper and record producer, Eminem, has solidified his place as an enduring figure in the world of rap music as his music video for the song ‘Rap God’ has crossed the milestone of one billion views on the video platform Youtube.

The video, which was initially launched in 2013, had amassed over 990,000 views by last Friday before Eminem, who is 47 years old, pushed it further by sharing links to the video on his social media profiles, urging fans to watch it.

“Rap God” becomes the third music video by Eminem to surpass 1 billion views, with his tracks “Not Afraid” and “Love the Way You Lie,” featuring Rihanna, having also achieved this feat. As of Monday, Luis Fonsi and Daddy Yankee’s “Despacito” holds the record for the most-watched video on YouTube, with over 6.6 billion views.
